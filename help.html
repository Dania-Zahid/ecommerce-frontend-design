Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, services, and policies.

How do I track my order? +

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number on our website or the carrier's website to track your package in real-time.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We also offer financing options through Affirm for eligible purchases.

How can I change or cancel my order? +

If your order hasn't been processed for shipping yet, you can request changes or cancellation by contacting our customer service team immediately. Once an order has shipped, you'll need to wait until you receive it and then initiate a return if needed.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. During checkout, you'll see the available shipping options and costs for your location.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and with all tags attached. Some items may be final sale or have different return policies, which will be clearly marked on the product page.

Shipping Information

We offer various shipping options to meet your needs. Below you'll find detailed information about our shipping policies and options.

Shipping Options

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 3-5 business days $5.99 (Free on orders over $50)
Expedited Shipping 2 business days $12.99
Overnight Shipping 1 business day $24.99

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

International Shipping

International orders may be subject to customs fees, import duties, and taxes, which are the responsibility of the customer. These charges vary by country and are not included in the item price or shipping cost.

Returns Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with an item, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange.

Return Process

  1. Initiate your return through our Returns Center or contact customer service
  2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging with all tags attached
  3. Include the completed return form that came with your order
  4. Ship the package using the provided return label or your preferred carrier

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused, unworn, and in their original condition
  • Original tags must be attached and packaging included
  • Proof of purchase is required
  • Certain items like personal care products and underwear may not be returnable

Refunds

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5-10 business days after we receive and process your return. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Exchanges

For exchanges, please return the original item following the return process and place a new order for the item you'd like instead. This ensures you receive your new item as quickly as possible.
